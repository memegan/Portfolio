In 2016, I entered my artwork into the Fresno County Fair. My two watercolour paintings both recieved a first place award.

These images, I painted in Photoshop while I had some free time. I like them because each of them express a feeling, whether than be a cool icy feeling, or a warm beachy vibe.

These images I hand painted with either acrylic paints or watercolor. They took me anywhere from 30 minutes to 1 hour. I had to use patience and self-dicipline to get these done. I am really proud of them.
